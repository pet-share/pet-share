What?

This website is for pet-owners and veterinarians around the globe to input information regarding their pets. Pet Share allows you compare and contrast information about your beloved pets.

Our Motivation:

One of our founding members dog was unfortunately bitten twice by the same dog and suffered harsh injuries. With no ability to transfer information, the dog went scot-free and ended up hurting more and more dogs.What if we could warn people to stay away from the dog? We wondered what if the dog had rabies? Has the dog been vaccinated? How can we help this dog? The answer to all these questions is "Pet Share" The new revolutionary webiste to solve all your pet problems.

How?

You can insert information about your pet into the website. First click the "pet application" tab under the logo. You will directed to a form where you will get your ID. DO NOT FORGET THIS, this is to locate you pet later. After submitting your page you will be sent to a page. There you can either look at your form via the link or submit another form. The Application sheet underneath will help you find your pet.

Our Mission:

Our goal is to update our database with accurate names of pets and their diseases for veterinarians and pet enthusiasts to know the medical history of the pet.
